About Ronnie Hall

Ever since I was young child I have often dreamed of teaching and inspiring the world through music. Music is such a fulfilling joy and I believe it is the universal language to everyone's heart and soul. For this reason, I am extremely passionate about working with my vocalist. Every person has a song in their heart and my goal as an instructor is to help students outwardly reflect their song.

I am honored to work with

I received my Bachelor of Arts degree from Stephen F. Austin State University. I have worked with numerous Grammy award winners and local Austin bands. I have also worked as a vocal instructor for Zach Scott Theatre, A Clinician Instructore for U.I.L, and a Guest Clinician for the Austin Womens Chorus.
